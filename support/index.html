Frequently Asked Questions

Is USPass the official USCIS app?

No. USPass is an independent educational tool. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to USCIS or the U.S. Government.

Are the 128 questions the same as the real test?

Yes — the 128 official USCIS civics questions in the app are reproduced verbatim from USCIS's public-domain 2025 study materials. The app adds 172 additional original practice questions across the same 8 categories.

How does the practice exam match the real test?

The exam simulator shows 20 questions drawn proportionally from all 8 categories. You need 12 correct to pass, matching the USCIS 2025 format. There is no time limit.

What's included in the free version?

The 128 official USCIS civics questions across all 8 categories, the full 20-question practice exam (unlimited), the readiness score, quick practice (5 questions), and category-by-category study.

What do I get with Premium ($6.99)?

The 172 additional expanded practice questions (300 total), full spaced repetition (SM-2) scheduling, smart daily sessions, and full statistics with charts.

Is the purchase a subscription?

No — it is a one-time, non-consumable in-app purchase. You pay once and it is yours, restorable on any device signed in with the same Apple ID.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app, go to Settings → Upgrade to Premium → Restore Purchase, and follow the prompt. You must be signed in with the Apple ID that made the original purchase.

Can I get a refund?

All refunds are handled directly by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and request a refund for the USPass Premium purchase. See Apple's refund policy for details.

Does USPass collect my data?

No. All study progress, exam history, and preferences are stored locally on your device. No analytics, no trackers, no advertising. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Why do I need to pick a state?

Four USCIS civics questions depend on your state (governor, U.S. senators, capital, representative). Selecting your state lets the app show the correct answer for you. Your selection is stored only on your device.

The "current president" answer seems outdated.

USPass ships with the officeholders current as of the most recent release. If an official changes, we push a content update in the next app version. Email us if you notice anything stale.

How do I reset all progress?

Open the app, go to Settings → Reset All Progress. This permanently deletes all local study data, exam history, and spaced repetition schedules.

What iOS version do I need?

iOS 17.0 or later. USPass runs on iPhone and iPad.

I found a factual error in a question.

Please email us at kotliarenkostudio@gmail.com with the question text and the correction. We take accuracy seriously and will fix verified errors in the next release.

Is USPass available in Spanish?

Not yet, but it is on the roadmap for v1.1. The actual USCIS civics test is administered in English, but we plan to localize the app UI to support study in other languages.
