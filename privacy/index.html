Privacy Policy

Effective: 9 April 2026 · Last updated: 9 April 2026

USPass ("the app") is developed by Mykola Kotliarenko. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Summary

USPass does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All your study progress, exam results, and preferences are stored locally on your device and never transmitted to us or any third party.

Data Controller

The data controller for the purposes of the applicable privacy laws is:

Mykola Kotliarenko (sole trader)
Email: kotliarenkostudio@gmail.com

We are not required to appoint a Data Protection Officer under applicable privacy law.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers or fingerprints
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No crash reports sent to third parties
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No use of the App Tracking Transparency framework or IDFA

Lawful Basis for Processing

USPass does not collect or process any personal data. All data generated through your use of the app is stored exclusively on your device and is not accessible to us. As no personal data processing occurs by us as the data controller, no lawful basis under Article 6 of the applicable privacy law is required.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Study progress: which questions you have answered, your accuracy, and spaced repetition schedules
  • Exam history: scores, dates, and time taken for each practice exam
  • User preferences: appearance mode, test date, study reminder time
  • Custom flashcards: any flashcards you create within the app

This data is not accessible to us or any third party. It is not transmitted over the internet.

Data Retention

All app data is stored locally on your device for as long as the app is installed. We do not retain any data on servers or cloud systems. If you delete the app, the data in the app's sandboxed storage is removed by iOS. Note that data may persist in device backups (iCloud or local backups) managed by Apple, which are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

USPass does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

The only external communication the app makes is through Apple's StoreKit framework for in-app purchases. Apple (not us) receives purchase transaction data including Apple ID and payment information. We receive only an anonymised purchase receipt confirming whether premium has been unlocked. This is handled entirely by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

We do not transfer any personal data outside the United States. Apple may process purchase-related data internationally in accordance with their own privacy policy and data transfer mechanisms.

Children's Privacy

USPass is rated 4+ on the App Store. It does not knowingly collect any data from children or anyone else. The app is designed with privacy by default — it does not profile users of any age, does not implement age estimation, and does not use data in ways that could be detrimental to children. The app complies with the principles of the applicable children's privacy requirements.

Your Rights Under applicable privacy law

Under the applicable privacy laws and the relevant data protection legislation, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing of, and port your personal data, as well as the right to object to processing.

Since USPass does not collect, process, or store any personal data on our servers or systems, these rights are not exercisable against us — we hold nothing to access, correct, or delete.

You can delete all locally stored data at any time using the "Reset All Progress" option in the app's Settings, or by deleting the app from your device.

If you believe your data protection rights have been infringed, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority:

  • Website: the relevant authority's website
  • Telephone: see relevant authority

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date. Since we do not collect contact information, we cannot notify you directly — please check this page if you have concerns.
